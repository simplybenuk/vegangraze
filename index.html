The Vegan Graze app is no more

I'm really sorry, but I have decided to close the Vegan Graze app, as I no longer have the time to keep it updated. With the boom in Veganism over the last year or so, I'm confident that Vegan Graze is no longer needed anymore.

When I started the app, it was because I had turned vegan and was finding it really difficult to find a vegan sandwich on my local highstreet. That no longer seems to be a problem as all the major retailers have jumped on board and now there is at least one option on the menu for us to try

Thanks for all you're support. If you're interested in any of my other projects, please visit my website simplyben.co.uk
